About Me

Advocate Ajeet Kumar has been practicing law since 2004, specializing in both civil and criminal cases. With extensive experience representing clients in lower courts across Jehanabad, Arwal, and Patna, as well as in the High Court of Patna, he is known for his dedication, professionalism, and commitment to justice.

Currently serving as an Executive Member of the Bar Council, Jehanabad, Advocate Ajeet Kumar combines deep legal expertise with a client-focused approach, ensuring that every case receives meticulous attention and strategic guidance.

Whether navigating complex legal matters or providing practical legal advice, Advocate Ajeet Kumar is committed to protecting your rights and achieving the best possible outcomes.
